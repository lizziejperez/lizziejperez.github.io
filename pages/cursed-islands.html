Game Description

Cursed Islands is a third-person 3D platformer built in Unity and C#. Navigate an outdoor island environment, collect gold coins, use pickups to stay alive, and adapt to new threats after the curse begins.

Core loop: Explore → platform → collect → manage health/time → survive the curse.

Highlights

Moving Paths

Health Pickups

The Curse Phase

After the curse begins, enemies and hazards intensify—avoid ghosts and slime to survive.

Treasure-focused level design encourages route planning and risk/reward decisions while exploring.
